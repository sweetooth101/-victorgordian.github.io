What is SDLC?

The SDLC is a structured process for building software from idea to deployment and maintenance. Typical phases:

  1. Planning – scope, stakeholders, feasibility
  2. Requirements – functional/non-functional specs
  3. Design – architecture, data models, UI flows
  4. Implementation – coding the solution
  5. Testing – verifying correctness and quality
  6. Deployment – releasing to users
  7. Maintenance – fixes, updates, improvements

Why it matters

  • Reduces risk and surprises
  • Improves quality and predictability
  • Helps teams coordinate across roles
